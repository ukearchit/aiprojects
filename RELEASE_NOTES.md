## 🎯 Overview

Version 1.0.0 represents the foundational release of a next-generation regulatory intelligence research platform. This release establishes the architectural framework and research roadmap for evolving static neuro-symbolic compliance systems into dynamic, stateful regulatory intelligence engines capable of serving trillion-dollar financial and insurance markets.

## 🔍 Problem Statement

**Evolving Static Neuro-Symbolic Compliance into Dynamic, Stateful Regulatory Intelligence**

The primary challenge: while the Decoupled Neuro-Symbolic Agentic Architecture (Translate-Verify-Narrate pipeline) has solved the LLM hallucination crisis, current implementations remain fundamentally static, stateless, and binary—operating as passive gatekeepers rather than active deal-makers in high-value commercial markets.

## ✨ What's Included in v1.0.0

### Core Architecture
- **Translate-Verify-Narrate Pipeline Framework**: Foundation for decoupled LLM and mathematical verification systems
- **Mathematical Verification Engine**: Architecture for generating legally defensible, proof-carrying audit trails
- **Compliance Evaluation Framework**: Structured approach to constraint satisfaction and rule evaluation

### Research Foundation
- **Regulatory CI/CD Skeleton**: Framework for continuous regulatory mandate translation
- **Stateful Portfolio Verification**: Architecture for real-time portfolio connectivity
- **Compliant Solution Synthesis**: Constraint relaxation mechanisms for alternative structuring
- **Red-Teaming Framework**: Adversarial testing structure for compliance systems
- **M2M Regulatory Interfaces**: Machine-to-machine regulatory communication specifications

### Technology Foundation
